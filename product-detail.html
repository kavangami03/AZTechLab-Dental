Product Name

Crown

Elevating Restoration Standards

Our Premium Porcelain Crowns represent the pinnacle of digital dentistry and artistic craftsmanship. Engineered for both exceptional durability and lifelike aesthetics, these restorations are designed to integrate seamlessly with the patient's natural dentition.

At AZTech Dental Lab, we utilize advanced CAD/CAM technology combined with hand-layered finishing to ensure a marginal fit of surgical precision and a color match that satisfies the most demanding cosmetic cases.

Key Technical Advantages

  • High Translucency: Natural light-diffusing properties for superior aesthetics.
  • Extreme Durability: Sintered at high temperatures for long-lasting performance.
  • Biocompatible: 100% metal-free materials, gentle on gingival tissues.
  • Digital Precision: Precision-milled to within 10 microns for perfect occlusion.

Clinical Applications

Beyond standard single-unit cases, our crowns are indicated for complex cosmetic rehabilitations, implant-supported restorations, and high-stress posterior situations where both strength and beauty are non-negotiable.

Preparation Metrics

Material Composition Marginal Prep Occlusal Reduction
High-Purity Zirconia 1.0 - 1.2 mm 1.5 - 2.0 mm
Lithium Disilicate 0.8 - 1.0 mm 1.0 - 1.5 mm

Clinical Advisory

For optimal results, we suggest a minimum reduction of 1.5mm on the incisal/occlusal edge to allow for proper material thickness and shade characterization.

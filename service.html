Our Services

Crown

Porcelain-Fused-to-Metal (PFM) Crown: - we use a traditional approach towards balancing out the strength (metal framework) and the natural appearance (porcelain exterior).

Bridges

Cantilever Bridges - we use zirconia and PFM to make this bridge. Not recommended for the back of the mouth where they put too much force on other teeth and damage them.

Implants

Custom Abutments - We fabricate using CAD/CAM processes which allow for high precision and consistency. The materials used are titanium, zirconia, or a combination.

Removable

Full Dentures - These are all made using Acrylic, which is available in multiple colors. For the Denture we also offer custom tray, occlusion tray and combination of both.

AZTech Dental Lab: A New Era of Precision and Partnership

Published On: April 17, 2026

Table Of Contents

We are proud to announce our latest facility expansion, integrating state-of-the-art CAD/CAM systems to better serve our dental partners across the region. This expansion marks a significant milestone in our 60-year history of excellence. Our commitment to precision and partnership has never been stronger.

As we embrace the future of dental technology, our new facility is equipped with the latest diagnostic tools and fabrication systems, ensuring that every restoration we produce meets the highest standards of accuracy and aesthetics.

Our Commitment to Excellence

For over 60 years, AZTech Dental Lab has been a trusted partner for dental professionals. This expansion is not just about new machines; it's about elevating the standard of care we provide collectively with our dental partners.

We believe that by investing in technology, we are investing in the success of our dental partners. Our team of expert technicians is now empowered with tools that bridge the gap between traditional craftsmanship and digital precision.

Advancements in Precision Milling

The core of our expansion lies in our new high-speed 5-axis milling units. These machines represent the pinnacle of dental fabrication, allowing us to process exotic materials like Translucent Zirconia and PEEK with sub-micron accuracy. This means tighter margins, better fit, and significantly reduced chairside adjustment time for clinicians.

By leveraging these advancements, we can now offer ultra-thin veneers and complex multi-unit bridges that was previously difficult to achieve with traditional methods. The result is a restoration that not only looks natural but functions with the durability that modern patients expect.

The Clinical Advantage

For the practicing dentist, this technological shift translates to clinical confidence. When you send us a digital impression, our systems analyze the 3D data to identify potential undercuts or clearance issues before we even begin the design phase. This proactive approach eliminates the back-and-forth often associated with complex restorative cases.

Digital Workflow Integration

Transitioning to a digital workflow can be daunting, but AZTech is here to act as your digital navigator. Our laboratory is now fully integrated with cloud-based platforms that allow for real-time file sharing and instant communication between the dental chair and the design technician.

This seamless connectivity ensures that every detail—from shade matching to occlusal preferences—is captured and executed with robotic precision. We are not just making crowns; we are digitizing the entire restorative experience to be more efficient and predictable.

A Sustainable Future

Beyond precision, our new facility focuses on sustainability. Digital dentistry significantly reduces the environmental impact of dental labs by eliminating the need for chemical stone models and traditional impression materials. Our Eco-Model System utilizes recyclable resins and biodegradable packaging, aligning our laboratory with the green initiatives of modern dental practices.

We invite you to experience the AZTech difference. Whether you are a long-time partner or considering your first digital case, our team is ready to provide the precision and partnership that has defined us for over six decades.

The Clinical Advantage

“Innovation is the heartbeat of our lab, and precision is our signature. We build more than crowns; we build long-term clinical success.”

— Nick Conicella, Founder

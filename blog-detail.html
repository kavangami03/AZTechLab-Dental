AZTech Dental Lab: A New Era of Precision and Partnership

Published On: April 17, 2026

We are proud to announce our latest facility expansion, integrating state-of-the-art CAD/CAM systems to better serve our dental partners across the region. This expansion marks a significant milestone in our 60-year history of excellence. Our commitment to precision and partnership has never been stronger.

As we embrace the future of dental technology, our new facility is equipped with the latest diagnostic tools and fabrication systems, ensuring that every restoration we produce meets the highest standards of accuracy and aesthetics.

The Future of Digital Dentistry

As we transition into this new era, the integration of advanced digital workflows is at the core of our operations. From 3D scanning to precision milling, every step is optimized for superior patient outcomes.

  • 01 State-of-the-art CAD/CAM integration
  • 02 Enhanced precision in multi-unit restorations
  • 03 Digital shade matching and simulation

Our Commitment to Excellence

For over 60 years, AZTech Dental Lab has been a trusted partner for dental professionals. This expansion is not just about new machines; it's about elevating the standard of care we provide collectively with our dental partners.

“Innovation is the heartbeat of our lab, and precision is our signature. We build more than crowns; we build long-term clinical success.”

— Nick Conicella, Founder

We believe that by investing in technology, we are investing in the success of our dental partners. Our team of expert technicians is now empowered with tools that bridge the gap between traditional craftsmanship and digital precision.
